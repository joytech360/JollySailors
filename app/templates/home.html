About Us

At Care Connect, we're dedicated to connecting parents to daycare providers in the city for on demand care services. We understand that emergencies happen, and we're here to make sure you have the support you need. Our app allows you to find a provider in your area, read reviews from other parents, and book on demand care for your child. With CareConnect, you can have peace of mind knowing that your child is in good hands.
